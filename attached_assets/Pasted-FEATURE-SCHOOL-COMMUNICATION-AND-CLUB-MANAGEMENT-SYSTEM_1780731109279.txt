1. SCHOOL BRANDING SYSTEM
   ==================================================

Admin should manage school branding.

Admin can upload:

* School logo
* School name
* School motto
* School contact information
* School address
* School email
* School website

Requirements:

* School logo appears in the header of every page.
* School name appears beside the logo.
* Branding updates automatically across the entire system.
* Store branding settings in the database.

Landing Page:

The landing page should prominently display:

* Large school logo
* Large school name
* School motto
* Welcome message

This should create a strong first impression for visitors.
